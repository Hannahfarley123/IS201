Floral Design Tips and Gallery

  1. Choosing ingredients for a flower arrangement
    • Choose focal flowers
      • Larger blooms
      • Often round
      • More visual weight
    • Line flowers
      • Taller flowers
      • Blooms forming along the stems
      • Used with greenery to make overall shape
    • Filler flowers
      • Smaller blooms
      • Add texture, depth, and color
    • Detain flowers
      • More delicate blooms
      • Add airiness and movement/li>
    • Greenery
      • Helps create shape
  2. Determining color palette
    • Monochramatic colors
      • Shades and tones of the same hue
    • Analagous colors
      • Colors next to each other on the color wheel
    • Complementary colors
      • Colors opposite each other on the color wheel
  3. Choosing ingredients for a flower arrangement
    • Placement of blooms
      1. Greenery
      2. Line flowers
      3. Big to small
